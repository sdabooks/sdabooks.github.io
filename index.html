Prison of the Storm Maiden

Echoes of Lost Merisolis

A warrior-lord rejected by his people, a cursed figurine, and a maiden from times long past thrust into an unfamiliar world!

In the depths of a forgotten mountain temple, an exiled warrior-lord from distant Ansharas stumbles upon a cursed agate figurine, imbued with the unpredictable magic of the forgotten trickster god. Hounded by the temple's monstrous guardians, the figurine leads him to a centuries-old secret — a slumbering maiden from the lost realm of Merisolis, the last of her kind, with the power of the tempest at her command.

As they join forces to escape the treacherous corridors of the temple, their destinies intertwine. With his swordsmanship and her control over the forces of nature, they navigate the dangers of the ancient vaults. Will the Chaos Lord favor them with freedom, or will their fiery passions bind them in a stormy prison of desire?

Delve into the enchanting world of Prison of the Storm Maiden to uncover their fates!

Contains some strong language, steamy scenes, scenes of grief, and a guaranteed HEA. For mature audiences.

Adventure

High in the secluded, snow-capped peaks of the forsaken mountains, the ancient temple stands shrouded in mystery, its desolate corridors echoing with secrets and guarded by ruthless monsters and golems. Within these hallowed, forgotten walls, fabulous treasures and enigmatic enchanted mechanisms await discovery, and the treacherous passageways draw our heroes deeper into a world of peril and enchantment with each step!

Romance

At the heart of the story lies a romance both tender and intense. The solitary survivor of the fallen Merisolis awakens tragically alone in an unfamiliar world. Both without a home, our heroes' shared loneliness becomes the crucible of an unbreakable bond, deepening into a love that transcends the boundaries of time. United by a sworn vow, their attraction ignites a fiery passion that will set your heart ablaze. Prison of the Storm Maiden is an emotionally charged, captivating romance that will leave you yearning for more.

Mystery

Abandoned by its builders in the distant past, the temple's shadowy facade conceals its own cryptic history. The cursed agate figurine, infused with the capricious essence of a trickster god, holds the promise of both blessings and curses, its magic an unpredictable force that could alter the course of our heroes' fate. And at the heart of it all is the maiden of ancient Merisolis, awakened from a slumber that spans centuries.
